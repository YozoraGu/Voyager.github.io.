FAQ


What platforms are compatible with SatBuilder?

SatBuilder is a web-app, so you can use it on any device with a supported web browser. As of our latest update, most features are only available for devices with a desktop or laptop screen size.


Is there a student or educational discount?

If you are a student, you can purchase the student plan which has all of the features you need to get started for a discounted price of $19/month. Please see our pricing page for more details.


Is Voyager hiring?

Voyager is not hiring at the moment, but we'll be sure to announce when we are! Create an account and subscribe to email updates to be notified about these changes!


Why, as a vendor, should I work with Voyager?

SatBuilder is a great way to allow satellite designers to integrate your parts in their design. Send us your component specifications and we will add them to our component database. Designers will then be free to try out your components in their designs. Posting your components on SatBuilder can also provide your business with valuable customer use statistics and feedback.
